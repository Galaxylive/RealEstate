Individual preferences, combined

Even though we have a lot in common with our partners, we value things differently when searching for the right home. David may prefer a large workshop space and big kitchen, while Sandy may care most about distance to entertainment and commute to work. Homezzo records all the unique preferences each person has and then asks each partner to rate their relative importance. The result is a powerful measure of shared preferences.

Easily rate the important features of each home.

Homezzo provides homebuyers the opportunity to rate how well each home performs relative to each couple’s shared preferences. You can even add and rate new preferences on the fly, as looking at the unique features of a particular home may reveal hidden desires.

See which homes come out on top.

Our easy to interpret report includes a grade and score for each home you’ve rated together! Scores are derived from each couple’s shared preferences and how each home stacks up relative to them.

Keep track of the houses you love.

You can easily see all the homes under consideration, and Homezzo lets you easily add, delete, or change the descriptions for any of them.

Take us with you.

Our handy Android app lets you add new homes, rate their importance, and see how they stack up relative to the homes you’ve already rated. This makes Homezzo your ultimate home shopping companion on the go!
